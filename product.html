By Ayaan Irshad Sheikh (Age 14)
At just 14 years old, I have developed a sustainable and affordable Smart Crop Monitoring System designed to help farmers care for their crops more efficiently. Powered by a solar panel, the system uses an Arduino Uno to control multiple sensors that monitor soil moisture, temperature, and humidity, ensuring optimal crop health.

An ultrasonic sensor detects the presence of harmful insects, activating a servo-controlled spray mechanism to repel them without overusing chemicals. The system connects to a mobile app, allowing real-time monitoring and control from anywhere.
